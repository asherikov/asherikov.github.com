About

Currently, I am a PhD student at BIPOP team (INRIA, Greboble, France), where I am working on control of humanoid robots under supervision of Pierre-Brice Wieber and Dimitar Dimitrov. I obtained my master's degree from Örebro University in 2012. The subject of the master's thesis was model predictive control of a humanoid robot, refer to this page for more information. After gradutation I participated in SAUNA project at Örebro University as a developer of a trajectory tracking controller for an autonomous forklift truck.
